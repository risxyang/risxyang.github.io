themeing, designing, logistics! we are currently planning for our october event, so this writeup is under construction.

This year, I wanted to *revitalize* yhack's visual branding, swapping our old blue/white color scheme for dramatic purples and night sky blues. I settled on a theme of space exploration, with rockets and stars aplenty.

our logo

Each year, YHack tends to modify its logo a bit, and this year, I created a spaceship-looking design with a prominent Y in the middle. The font I used for all text was DIN Alternate.
