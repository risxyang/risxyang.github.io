yhack sponsorship rebranding

adobe photoshop cc 2018

To kick off this year's fundraising for YHack 2019, I redesigned our sponsorship brochure. Companies seeking to sponsor our hackathon will reference this document as they consider their desired degree of financial involvement.

In designing this brochure I emphasized clarity and compactness, as companies new to YHack will receive their first impressions of the event through the visuals of the brochure. In choosing the central graphic (a photo I took of YHack 2018) I wanted to convey the scope of the event while showcasing our venue. In light of Yale's reputation as a Humanities-centric school, I hoped the visual would redefine (or reinforce, if positive) the perception these companies have about the energy and excitement surrounding Yale STEM.
